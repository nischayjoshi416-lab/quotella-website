## Revenue Streams

### 1. Google AdSense (Primary - 80% of revenue)

**Setup:**
1. Apply at google.com/adsense
2. Wait for approval (1-2 weeks typically)
3. Add your Publisher ID to all HTML files
4. Update ads.txt file

**Expected Revenue:**
- **RPM (Revenue per 1000 visits):**
  - USA traffic: $3-8
  - UK traffic: $2-6
  - Global traffic: $1-4

- **Monthly Projections:**
  - 10,000 visitors ‚Üí $30-80
  - 50,000 visitors ‚Üí $150-400
  - 100,000 visitors ‚Üí $300-800
  - 500,000 visitors ‚Üí $1,500-4,000

**Optimization Tips:**
- Place ads above the fold
- Use responsive ad units
- Test different ad sizes
- Enable auto ads
- Monitor heat maps
- A/B test placements

**Best Ad Placements:**
1. Header (Leaderboard 728x90)
2. Sidebar (Medium Rectangle 300x250)
3. In-content (Native ads)
4. Between quotes (In-feed)
5. Footer (Responsive)
6. Mobile sticky bottom

## Maximizing AdSense Revenue

### Best Practices:
1. **Ad Placement:**
   - Above the fold (immediate view)
   - Between content (natural breaks)
   - End of articles (high engagement)

2. **Ad Types:**
   - Responsive display ads
   - In-feed native ads
   - In-article ads
   - Matched content

3. **User Experience:**
   - Don't overwhelm with ads
   - Balance content and ads
   - Mobile-friendly ads
   - Fast loading pages

4. **Traffic Quality:**
   - USA/UK/Canada = highest CPM
   - Organic > Social traffic
   - Engaged users = more clicks
   - Long session duration

### What NOT to Do:
- ‚ùå Click your own ads (ban risk)
- ‚ùå Ask users to click (ban risk)
- ‚ùå Too many ads (poor UX)
- ‚ùå Misleading ad placement
- ‚ùå Adult/illegal content
