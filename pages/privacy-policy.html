Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2024

QuotesVault collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site.

  • Local Storage: Your saved favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we store your email and message.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To analyze site usage and trends
  • To send newsletter (only if you subscribe)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

4. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Advertising personalization (Google AdSense)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of newsletter emails
  • Disable cookies

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
